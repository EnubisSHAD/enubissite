Our Mission

Enubis’s goal is to reduce the amount of e-waste that ends up in our landfills by giving the used devices a new life.
Enubis wants to make proper disposal of e-waste the norm and also provide high quality refurbished smart-phones to the financially conscious.


The entire world is in the midst of an electronic waste crisis, with more and more technology produced, but disposed of incorrectly. Yet, there has been no company that has stepped up to the challenge of finding an effective way to dispose of e-waste properly until us. Enubis, a group of bright-minded teenagers will provide an easy way to recycle your broken or unwanted technology. With the passion for entrepreneurship and innovation from all across Canada working on this threat, Enubis can seriously reduce the amount of wrongly disposed of e-waste and give it a new life.

Our service is a new way to properly recycle your old devices, it is easy, fast, modern and socially responsible. Due to the lack of awareness and the inconvenience of recycling e-waste not many people do it so it is left somewhere in the house collecting dust or it is simply tossed in the garbage where it is brought to a landfill where it will take 1-2 million years to fully decompose. Enubis has the potential to change the way the average person sees e-waste recycling and make it easier for them to be a responsible member of society. Our service takes in old or used phones in exchange for a small incentive ranging from 5-10 dollars. From there, we either recycle and properly dispose of the materials and precious metals inside of the phone or we refurbish them using parts from other donated phones. After the refurbishing process, we sell the working phones to less fortunate families essentially tapping into a nearly untouched market.
